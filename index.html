Projects

Unsupervised Speaker Count

Gururaj Krishnamurthy,Vijayakrishna Naganoor,Dr.Deepu Vijayaseenan
(Being Reviewed in IEEE International Conference on Multimedia and Expo, 2017)
Counting the number of speakers present in a given conversation is a challenging task. It can help in improving speaker diarization and in audio forensics. We are exploring this task using features generated from DCNNs and CRBMs to compare their effectiveness over conventional features like MFCCs. (Paper)

Music Genre Recognition

Vijayakrishna Naganoor, K.B. Rahul
Though genre classification is a standard task in MIR, a large scale version is hitherto unexplored to the best of our knowledge. We have attempted for classifying the songs not only into the right Genres but also its subgenre using Hierarchical classification.We have also explored the usage of Deep Convolutional Neural Networks in large scale genre classification. Our motivation stems from the unprecedented success of these methods in the field of computer vision for object classification. (Dataset)
