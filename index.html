I am a Machine Learning Engineer at KLA. I graduated with a Master's Student in Electrcical and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. My techincal intersts can be broadly classified as Machine Learning applications in Signal Processing and Data Analysis.

I have previously worked with Robert Laganiere in University of Ottawa on vehicle detection systems used for driver assistance and smart video surveillance applications.I have also interned in Vision and Speech Lab in IIIT Hyderabad where I worked on NIST I-vector Language Identification challenge. I worked towards my bachelor thesis under Dr. Prasanta Kumar Ghosh in Indian Institute of Science on analysis of the Indian Spoken English Pronunciation using rhythmic and prosodic cues.

Apart from this technical experience, I bring in with me a complete package of a 'Semi-classical singer', 'Convincing actor', 'Good orator' and above all, an 'Engaging Story-teller'. I like my audience big and friends circle small.
